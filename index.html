Our research in theoretical high energy physics focus on quantum field theory and its application, with main research focuses on:

  • Infrared structure of non-abelian gauge theories
  • Effective field theory
  • QCD factorization theorem
  • All-order resummation and parton shower
  • Collider phenomenology: jet, heavy flavor and Higgs physics
  • Spin physics and quantum tomography of nucleons
  • Application of quantum information theory
  • AI for renormalization group flow

The research is closely connected to the experiments performed at the large hadron collider (LHC), relativistic heavy ion collider (RHIC), the future electron-ion collider (EIC) and super tau charm factory (STCF).

If you are interested in working with us as a postdoc, graduate student, or undergrad, please contact us.
